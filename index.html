Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our billing, technology, and treatments.

Do you accept my dental PPO insurance plan? ▼

Yes! We accept a wide range of dental PPO insurance providers, including Aetna, Delta Dental, MetLife, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and Cigna. Our clinical front desk staff will handle all claims processing directly to ensure you maximize your benefits.

What financing options do you provide? ▼

We are fully certified partners with CareCredit, which offers flexible, interest-free monthly financing plans for clinical treatments. We also provide customized in-house payment options to accommodate cosmetic veneers and aligners.

How long do Invisalign® treatments take? ▼

On average, orthodontic Invisalign® corrections take between 6 to 18 months, depending on your individual bite configuration. We supply digital smile renderings during your consult so you can view your expected results beforehand!

What is your emergency dental care procedure? ▼

We hold designated daily emergency blocks for patients suffering from severe tooth pain, chips, or unexpected fractures. Call our Oram Street office at (214) 823-1638 directly for immediate same-day priority appointments.
